### 1. Abstract
The GSL Engine is a proprietary, closed-source heuristic solver designed for the CVRP. This paper highlights its breakthrough in computational efficiency—solving massive-scale instances (up to 10,000 nodes) on a mobile device. The results confirm that GSL’s targeted logic achieves highly competitive Gaps compared to Best Known Solutions (BKS) in classical datasets.

### 2. Execution Environment & Integrity
To prove the engine's efficiency, the entire benchmark suite was executed under the following "Edge" conditions:

* **Hardware:** Standard Mobile Device (ARM-based Snapdragon Processor).
* **Software:** Android OS via Pydroid 3 (Python 3.x).
* **Zero-Tuning Policy:** 
  * One unified script (V22) for all 10,000+ files.
  * No instance-specific parameter adjustment.
  * No manual "cheating" or overfitting to specific datasets.

### 4. Technical Strategy: Why GSL is Faster
The engine does not rely on brute-force. It uses Targeted Heuristic Intelligence:

* **Geometric Spatial Sensing:** Pre-calculates route density to prune the search space before execution.
* **Deterministic Fleet Minimization:** Focuses on reducing the number of vehicles (K) to maximize logistical savings.
* **Memory Footprint Optimized:** Operates within strict mobile RAM limits (<500MB) even for massive instances.
